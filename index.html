Earning tokens
Every £1.50 paid toward your debt earns 1 token. Pay your minimum of £50 per week and earn 75 tokens.

Overpayment bonus
Pay more than your weekly minimum and earn 2x tokens on the amount above minimum. Rewarding excess. Encouraging sacrifice.
